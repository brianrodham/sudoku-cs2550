How to play

The objective of sudoku is to fill a 9x9 grid so that each row, column, and 9x9 subgrid each contain each of the numbers 1-9 exactly once. Bolded numbers are placed on the board at the beginning of the game to help you fill in the rest of the board. These numbers can not be changed.
